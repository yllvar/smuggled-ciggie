## New Disclaimer Section to Add

### Data Limitations and Disclaimers

"**Important Note on Data Sources and Limitations**

The analysis presented in this report is based on the Illicit Cigarettes Study (ICS) Malaysia January 2024 Report by Nielsen Consumer LLC. Key limitations include:

1. **Temporal Relevance**: All statistics reflect conditions as of January 2024 and may not represent current market conditions.

2. **Sampling Methodology**: Data is based on Nielsen's sampling approach and may not capture the full universe of illicit cigarette activity.

3. **Theoretical Projections**: Enforcement scenario ROI calculations are model-based projections assuming optimal conditions.

4. **Comparative Claims**: Cross-country comparisons (e.g., Singapore) are based on reported figures and may not reflect current conditions.

5. **Economic Assumptions**: Financial impact calculations use fixed economic parameters that may not reflect current market prices or tax rates.

For the most current data, please consult the latest ICS Malaysia reports and official government statistics."
